EverGlow Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 9, 2026

Introduction

EverGlow ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how our mobile application handles your information.

Information We Collect

Information Stored Locally on Your Device

EverGlow stores all your data locally on your device. We collect and store:

  • Event Information: Event names, dates, times, and types (countdown/count-up)
  • Custom Images: Background images you choose for your events
  • App Preferences: Your settings and preferences within the app
  • Subscription Status: Whether you have purchased premium features

Information We Do NOT Collect

  • We do NOT collect any personal information
  • We do NOT track your location
  • We do NOT access your contacts
  • We do NOT collect analytics or usage data
  • We do NOT store your data on our servers
  • We do NOT sell or share any information with third parties

How We Use Your Information

All data you create in EverGlow is:

  • Stored locally on your device only
  • Used solely to provide the app's functionality
  • Never transmitted to our servers or any third party
  • Completely under your control

Photo Library Access

EverGlow requests access to your photo library for the following purposes:

  • To allow you to select background images for your events
  • To save shared event images to your photo gallery

Your photos are only accessed when you explicitly choose to:

  • Select a background image for an event
  • Save a shared event card to your gallery

We do not upload, store, or share your photos anywhere outside your device.

Notifications

EverGlow uses local notifications to:

  • Remind you of upcoming events
  • Alert you when countdowns reach zero

These notifications are generated entirely on your device. We do not collect any data from these notifications, and they are not sent through any external servers.

In-App Purchases

If you choose to purchase premium features:

  • Payment processing is handled entirely by Apple through the App Store
  • We do not collect or store your payment information
  • Subscription status is stored locally on your device
  • RevenueCat may be used to manage subscriptions (see their privacy policy)

Data Security

Since all your data is stored locally on your device:

  • Your data security depends on your device's security
  • We recommend using device passcode/Face ID/Touch ID
  • Data is automatically deleted if you uninstall the app
  • We cannot recover your data if lost

Children's Privacy

EverGlow does not knowingly collect any information from children under 13. The app is designed for general audiences and does not contain any content specifically directed at children.

Your Rights

You have complete control over your data:

  • Access: All your data is visible within the app
  • Modify: You can edit or delete any event at any time
  • Delete: Uninstalling the app permanently deletes all your data
  • Export: Currently, data export is not available (no cloud storage)

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by:

  • Posting the new Privacy Policy in the app
  • Updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy

Third-Party Services

RevenueCat (for subscription management)

  • Privacy Policy: revenuecat.com/privacy
  • Used only to manage in-app purchase subscriptions
  • Does not access your personal data or event information

Apple App Store

  • Privacy Policy: apple.com/legal/privacy
  • Handles all payment processing for in-app purchases

Contact Us

Email: openloop.studios@gmail.com

Summary

  • All your data stays on your device
  • We don't collect personal information
  • We don't track you
  • We don't sell your data
  • Photos are only used when you choose them
  • Notifications are local only
  • You have full control over your data

EverGlow is designed with privacy in mind. Your countdown moments are yours alone.
